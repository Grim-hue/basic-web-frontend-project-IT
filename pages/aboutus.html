About Us

Welcome to Echo Link, a platform designed to amplify your voice and connect you with a vibrant community. Whether you’re here to chat, share posts, or explore the thoughts and ideas of others, Echo Link is your digital space to express and engage.

Built by Two Dreamers

Echo Link was created by two passionate individuals, André and Carlos, with a shared vision: to create a simple, user-friendly platform where people can share their thoughts, start conversations, and stay connected. What started as a small idea between friends has grown into a space where anyone can feel heard and find their echo in the world.

Echo Link is a project developed as part of the ISTEC discipline.

Why Echo Link?

We believe everyone deserves a voice. That’s why we’ve combined the power of chat and posting into one seamless experience. With features that are easy to use and a community that’s ready to engage, Echo Link is here to make sharing your story effortless and meaningful.

Future Plans

Echo Link is just the beginning. We aim to continuously improve and expand the platform to provide even more engaging ways to connect. In the future, we plan to implement exciting new features like stories, allowing users to share short, time-sensitive updates and moments. Stay tuned as Echo Link evolves to better meet the needs of our growing community!
